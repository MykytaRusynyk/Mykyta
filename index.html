About Me

I am a Database Development specialist focused on creating reliable, structured, and high-load data architectures.

My core focus is Database Engineering. I design database schemas that don't fail under pressure and write T-SQL that scales. While I have background in Front-End development, my career and passion are dedicated to the back-end data layer: architecture, optimization, and ETL/BI logic.

Key focus areas:

  • Database Design: Relational modeling and schema architecture for high-concurrency systems.
  • Optimization: Deep-level query tuning, indexing strategies, and execution plan analysis.
  • ETL & BI: Building robust data movement pipelines (SSIS) and analytical models (SSAS, Power BI).
  • Automation: Implementing CI/CD for database deployments safely across environments.
  • Full-Stack Support: Leveraging front-end experience to build better data-driven interfaces when needed.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science and thrive in environments where data integrity and performance are mission-critical.
